About Ellie

I'm a college student with way too much yarn and a desire to create. Whether it's a hat shaped like your favorite character, a funky beanie for festivals, or a cozy hood for everyday wear — I love bringing creative ideas to life with a crochet hook.

“I love bringing creative ideas to life with a crochet hook.”

Every piece is made by hand with care and attention to detail. I work with my customers to design something truly one-of-a-kind — something that reflects your personality and style. From the yarn selection to the final stitch, each project is a labor of love.

Whether you have a clear vision or just a spark of an idea, I'm here to help turn it into something you can actually wear. Let's make something awesome together.
